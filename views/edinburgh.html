FAQs

Is there a weight limit for my boxes or other items?

To make sure that your stuff is perfectly safe ‐ and out of concern for the backs of the collection and delivery team ‐ your boxes must not exceed 25kg, which is the same weight as the checked baggage allowance for most major airlines.

If you’ve heavier or larger items we may still be able to help. Select the appropriate item type in “Other Items” and where necessary include a description of the item including its weight and size.

What kind of boxes and packing materials do you provide?

The packing materials we provide are designed specifically for storage and transportation. Our cardboard boxes are made of double-walled corrugated cardboard for extra strength. We also sell specialist packing materials including boxes for suitcases, bikes, clothes, crockery and archiving. You can order your boxes, packing tape and bubble wrap by clicking here.
Here are some of the packing materials we sell:
Small box: 29 x 40 x 34cm Medium box: 41 x 41 x 41cm Large box: 45 x 45 x 50cm Medium suitcase box: 45 x 25 x 58cm Large suitcase box: 52 x 40 x 75cm Archive box: 40 x 34 x 29cm Book box: 40 x 34 x 29cm Crockery box: 36 x 58 x 32cm Clothes box: 46 x 33 x 33cm Bike box: 147 x 23 x 97cm
We really do recommend you use our boxes as they’re able to withstand significant weight and are suitable for transportation and storage. If you do need to use your own packing materials, please read through our guidelines here.

Do you dismantle furniture?

We won’t dismantle your furniture, so we recommend that you do that prior to your collection. However, we will pack and wrap your furniture securely to prepare it for transit and storage.
Our dedicated team can collect stuff that can fit through your doors, and that be safely carried by two people, from any room in your home.

When can you collect my stuff?

If you order before 11am, you can order your collection for Monday to Saturday, 3 working days from the booking date. If you also need packing and order before 11am, you can order 4 working days from the booking date. The collection is expected to begin at 9am but a member of our customer team will be in touch to confirm the date and time once you have booked.

What if I don’t know what unit size I want?

No problem, don’t worry just take your best guess when you book your collection. We will check and confirm once we’ve collected your stuff. If you end up storing more, we’ll simply update your price accordingly and let you know. When selecting your unit size, you can also click on ‘What fits in here?’ We’ll give you a rough idea of the unit size in ft3 or the total amount of LOVESPACE boxes you can store per unit. If you’re not sure what unit size you need, check out our calculator which will tell you which unit will suit you.

Is there anything I can’t store?

If you’re using our by-the-box service, we’ll store just about anything. We’re the UK’s storage experts so we also know what we can’t accept. Here’s a list of stuff that you mustn’t send in. Food and perishable goods including sauces (e.g. soy sauce and ketchup); Any liquids or aerosols Plastic bags, plastic boxes, bin bags, canvas bags and laundry bags unless these are packed inside cardboard boxes; Suitcases not packed in our suitcase boxes; Musical instruments in soft cases; Knives and sharp objects not suitably sleeved and wrapped; Mirrors or large glass items; Birds, fish, animals or any other living creature; Explosives, weapons or ammunition; Any item which emits fumes, smell or odour; Any illegal substances or goods illegally obtained; Combustible or flammable materials, liquids or compressed gases, including but not limited to: paint, diesel, petrol, oil, gas, alcohol, artificial fertiliser or cleaning solvents; Chemicals, radioactive materials, biological agents; Toxic waste, asbestos or other materials of a dangerous nature; Any other toxic, flammable or hazardous substance or preparations, classified as such under applicable law; Tobacco; Passports, cash, credit or debit cards. There are some things we may collect, but won’t cover. This means we don’t provide compensation coverage for them in the event of damage or loss. They include: Fragile stuff, including including crockery, glasses and mugs, unless they’re packed into our purpose-made LOVESPACE crockery boxes and thoroughly bubble-wrapped; Electrical items; Any item not packed inside a box. A £50 handling charge applies for any suitcases, holdalls, luggage, and sports bags we receive that aren’t inside a box. You can find a full list of what we won’t cover here.

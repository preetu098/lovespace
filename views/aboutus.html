Our story

LOVESPACE launched in 2011 as the UK’s first storage by-the-box service.
Our company was founded by Streetcar (the former car-sharing company) entrepreneur Brett Akker and the founder of City Self-Storage, Carl August Ameln.
We wanted to bring flexibility to storage, allowing each customer to scale up and pay only for the space they actually need. This disrupted the traditional storage industry, where customers have no choice but to buy a huge amount of space that they usually won’t fill.
In 2013, former group director of strategy at Guardian Media Group, Steve Folwell, joined as Managing Director, and we launched our first full service.

Over the past 10+ years, we’ve looked after thousands of UK college and university students’ stuff through our by-the-box service, collecting right from their doorstep and delivering back to wherever they are. But as it’s not just students who need their stuff looking after, in 2021 we launched our storage units, for customers with bulkier items that won’t fit in a box. Next came our first drive-up storage points for those with no time to waste and who want to drop their stuff off on their terms. In 2022 so far, we’ve introduced more drive-up storage points across London and have big plans to take them national. We’ve also launched our removals service – helping renters, homeowners and business owners make their moves. What’s in store next? Watch this space.

How it works

Book any of our services through our website and follow the step-by-step instructions.
Don’t know which service is best for you? Don’t worry. We can help recommend the best one.   

Storage by-the-box

Tell us the day you want us to collect your items and one of our friendly team will collect your stuff for free.
If you need packing materials, we can send those out to you beforehand too. You can even upload photos to your LOVESPACE account as a reminder of what's in each box.
We store your items in a secure storage facility for as long as you like. When you want any of your stuff back, place a booking through your LOVESPACE account, select your item(s) and we'll deliver back to you as soon as the next day.   

Storage units

Choose from five unit sizes based on the amount of stuff you have, tell us the day you want us to collect it and we’ll be there.
You can also add optional cardboard boxes at the same time or even pay for us to do all the packing for you.
We store your items in a secure storage facility for as long as you like. When you want any of your stuff back, place a booking through your LOVESPACE account, select your item(s) and we'll deliver back to you as soon as the next day.


We deliver.

Book online, then drop your stuff off by 11pm on your selected date, using your unique pin number. You can make multiple trips on your move-in day if you need to.
We’ll then take everything away to a secure storage facility til you need it back.
When it’s time to get your stuff back, log into your LOVESPACE account and we’ll deliver it to you as soon as two working days later.   





Removals.

Book a removal online, even at short notice. You’ll pay a single upfront fee, with additional disassembling, packing and wrapping if you need those too. We’ll collect your stuff and move it into your new space the same day.
